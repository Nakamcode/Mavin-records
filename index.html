Who Exactly Are We?

Mavin Records is the brain child of African renowned producer & entrepreneur, the Don Jazzy. Created as a wholly independent music label on the 8th of May, 2012; Mavin Records, immediately captured the world s attention.

Mavin is home to Africa's most exciting musical talents with dozens of awards, hit singles, brand endorsement and other numerous achievements...

Meet The Don Himself!

From his beginnings as a serial hit making producer to his transition into a music mogul with global dreams, Don's journey has come to define the expansion of African Music in the last decade.

Born Michael Collins Ajereh, better known as Don Jazzy, the singer, producer, song writer, and entrepreneur, is an Afro-beats Institution...

Music To The World!
